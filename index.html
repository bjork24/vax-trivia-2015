Answers

What is the name of the carnivorous plant that is primarily native to a 60-mile radius around Wilmington, North Carolina?

In the Scooby-Doo cartoons, what is the name of the gang’s van?

Mystery Machine

If you attend a potluck in Minneapolis and the host asks you to provide a “hotdish,” what type of food should you bring?

Casserole

What name did the media use to refer to the scandal that resulted in Tom Brady being suspended for the first four games on the 2016 season?

Deflategate

What is the name of Harper Lee’s second novel, which is slated to be released next month?

Go Set a Watchmen

In the Harry Potter universe, a game of Quidditch can only end after a player (typically the seeker) successfully catches what?

The golden snitch

This actor has provided the voices for Ned Flanders, Mr. Burns, and Smithers on the Simpsons for 26 years, but is threatening to leave the show over a contract dispute.

Harry Shearer

The tip of the Washington Monument is made out of what type of metal? At the time of its casting in 1884 it was largest single piece of this material in the world, measuring in at 8.9 inches tall and weighing 100 ounces.

Aluminum

This child star had his film debut as Forrest Junior in Forrest Gump and can be seen most recently in the new Entourage film.

Haley Joel Osment

Which planet in our solar system has a longer day (time to complete one rotation) than year (time to orbit the sun once)?

Venus - 243 Earth-day day / 225 Earth-day year
